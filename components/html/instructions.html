Instructions

  • Access the Calculator page by clicking on the "GPA Calculator" in the navigation menu.
  • On the GPA Calculator page, enter your grades, classes, and credits in the respective input fields.
  • After entering all necessary information, click on the "Calculate GPA" button.
  • View unweighted and weighted GPA, and adjust values accordingly.
  • Ask the Q&A chatbot if you have any questions!
